The CoffeeToffee Story

Coffee-flavored toffees are a type of candy that has a rich, creamy, and smooth texture with a strong coffee flavor. These toffees are typically made by boiling sugar, butter, and cream to a high temperature until it forms a thick, caramel-like consistency, and then adding coffee flavoring. The flavoring used in coffee-flavored toffees can vary, but it's often a combination of natural or artificial coffee extracts, powders, or oils. Some toffees may also contain actual coffee or espresso.

CoffeeToffee is a popular candy for coffee lovers and can be enjoyed as a sweet treat or as a way to satisfy a coffee craving.
