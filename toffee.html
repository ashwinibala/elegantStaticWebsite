Toffees

Coffee-flavored candy is a type of confectionery that is designed to taste like coffee. There are many different types of coffee-flavored candy, including hard candies, caramels, chocolates, and more.

Dark Roast Coffee Toffee: This type of toffees combines the rich flavor of chocolate with the distinct taste of coffee. They are available in many different forms, including addons and toppings to taste.
Dark Chocolate Coffee Toffee: This type of toffees combines the rich flavor of chocolate with the distinct taste of coffee. They are available in many different forms, including chocolate-covered coffee beans and chocolate bars with coffee flavoring.
Caramel Coffee Toffee: These candies have a soft, chewy texture and are made with a combination of coffee flavoring and caramel which are individually wrapped to perfection.
Coffee Peanut Toffee: These candies have a soft, chewy crunchy texture and are made with a combination of coffee flavoring and peanut which are individually wrapped to perfection.
Milk Coffee Toffee: These candies have a soft, chewy crunchy texture and are made with a combination of coffee and milk flavoring which are individually wrapped to perfection.

Overall, coffee-flavored candy is a great option for anyone who loves coffee and wants to enjoy its flavor in a different form.
